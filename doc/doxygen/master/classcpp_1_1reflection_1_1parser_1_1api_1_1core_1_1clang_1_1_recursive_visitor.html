Detailed Description

Implements a recursive AST visitor interface.

A recursive visitor implements a depth-first AST traversal that is guaranteed to visit all the nodes of a (sub)tree. The traversal can be aborted by returning Visitor::Result::Break from the onCursor() methods.

The onVisitor() methods of this visitor class are identified by the visitor_tags::Recursive tag.
